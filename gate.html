Product contract

A checkpoint between your LLM and your user.

Your product sends a user message, AI answer, business data, rules, and context. SemeAI Gate returns a business action: SHOW REVIEW or BLOCK.

Open live console Open dashboard Register workspace

SHOW

Maps to PROCEED. Supported enough for release.

REVIEW

Maps to NEEDS_REVIEW. Do not auto-release. Route to an operator.

BLOCK

Maps to SILENCE. Withhold the answer and preserve audit metadata.

Contract shape

Input

user_message, ai_answer, business_data, business_rules, business_context, business_risk, metadata

Output

action, internal_decision, show_to_user, reason, context_integrity, next_step, audit_id, audit_preserved

Invariant

Business labels may be localized. Machine values remain canonical for audit and replay. Payment is not gate authority.
