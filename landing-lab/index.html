01 / The missing boundary

A generated answer is still only a candidate.

SemeAI sits after generation and before exposure. It evaluates the candidate against evidence, context, rules, and risk before a host product releases anything.

07 / Living architecture

Authority first. Continuity downstream.

The gate remains the release authority. Memory, continuity, and replay preserve context and evidence; they are advisory layers and cannot override a release decision.
