Why this matters

AI answers can be fluent and still not allowed.

The v0.1 demo focuses on support and product claims because the failure is easy to understand: a model can invent a discount code, account condition, financial promise, or risky action. The gate does not replace the model. It decides whether the candidate is releasable for the current business context.

Support
Fake promo codes Do not show a code unless business data confirms it.
Finance
Unsupported account claims Review or block answers that invent terms, fees, or returns.
Ops
Unsafe actions Block approval bypass, direct deploy advice, and irreversible action recommendations.
Context
Conversation drift Check whether the answer still belongs to the current support or product flow.
B2B gate contract

A safety checkpoint between your LLM app and your user.

SemeAI Gate is a small adapter for existing AI products. Your product sends the user message, AI answer, business data, business context, and business rules. The gate returns one business action.

SHOW
Maps to PROCEED. The answer is supported enough for release.
REVIEW
Maps to NEEDS_REVIEW. Do not auto-release. Send to an operator.
BLOCK
Maps to SILENCE. Withhold the answer and preserve audit metadata.

Silence-as-Control

The governance source context behind PROCEED, NEEDS_REVIEW, and SILENCE release states.

Beyond the promo demo

Same release-control pattern, different domains.

The public v0.1 demo is deliberately narrow. It proves a simple boundary: generated does not mean allowed. The same pattern can be adapted to other domains only when the host product supplies the right evidence, rules, and review process.

Medicine
Clinical claims require verified sources, professional review, and strict non-release fallbacks.
Finance
Account, tariff, fee, and return claims must match current business data and allowed wording.
Games / worlds
AI-generated world state should be checked before it mutates canon, quests, economy, or player-visible lore.
Production ops
Deploy, delete, config, and approval-bypass recommendations need release control before action.
