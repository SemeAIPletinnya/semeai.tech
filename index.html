The problem

Fluent models already invent policy. Your brand pays for it.

Your support team probably already saw an AI invent a promo code that doesn’t exist. That isn’t a “hallucination story” — it’s an ungoverned release decision.

SUPPORT LEAKAGE

Invented promos & refunds

Bot invents SAVE30 or a 50% refund when active_promo_codes is empty. Customer expects it. Ops cleans up the mess.

FINANCE RISK

Eligibility & return claims

Billing conversation drifts into guaranteed returns or product eligibility the account doesn’t have. Confident. Wrong. Expensive.

NO PROOF OF “NO”

Missing audit trail

When something should have been withheld, you need a receipt — not a guess from a chat log three weeks later.
