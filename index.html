The problem

Fluent models invent policy.
Your brand pays for it.

Ungoverned AI doesn’t just “hallucinate” — it releases invented promos, refunds, and eligibility claims as if they were product truth.

SUPPORT LEAKAGE

Invented promos & refunds

Bot invents SAVE30 or a 50% refund when business data is empty. Customer expects it. Ops cleans the mess.

FINANCE RISK

Eligibility drift

Billing chat slides into guaranteed returns or products the account doesn’t have. Confident. Wrong. Expensive.

NO PROOF OF “NO”

Missing audit trail

When something should have been withheld, you need a receipt — not a guess from a chat log weeks later.
