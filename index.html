The hidden cost of ungoverned AI

Fluent models already invent policy. Your brand pays for it.

Every support and ops surface that “just answers” is a silent product decision. Without a gate, invented promos, refunds, and eligibility claims look authoritative.

Support leakage

Bots invent SAVE30 when active_promo_codes is empty — and customers expect it to work.

Finance risk

Eligibility and return claims drift off-context into confident, wrong advice.

No proof of “no”

When something should have been withheld, you need a receipt — not a chat log guess.
