How it works

Four steps. One decision. A receipt.

SemeAI is not another chatbot. It is a narrow checkpoint your product calls after generation.

1

Generate

Your existing LLM writes a candidate answer. Nothing is shown yet.

2

Check

Gate evaluates business data, rules, and conversation context.

3

Decide

SHOW, REVIEW, or BLOCK — mapped to PROCEED / NEEDS_REVIEW / SILENCE.

4

Prove

Even blocked answers leave audit metadata for replay and review.

Why teams need this

Fluent is not the same as safe to ship.

Without a gate

  • AI invents promo codes and refunds
  • Off-context answers drift into finance claims
  • Risky ops advice reaches users
  • No audit when something was blocked

With SemeAI Gate

  • Unsupported claims are withheld
  • Ambiguous cases go to REVIEW
  • Safe answers still ship as SHOW
  • Every decision can leave a receipt
