Unsupported claims

No universal AI-safety proof, AGI measure, hallucination guarantee, security certification, or compliance certification is claimed.

Retrieval is not truth. A commit is implementation evidence, not proof of idea origin. The Repository Evidence Benchmark admits bounded visible evidence and controls result display only.
